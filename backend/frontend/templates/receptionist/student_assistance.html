Student Assistance

Information guide for students and new enrollees

📋 Admissions Process
Step 1: Complete the application form (available at Front Office)
Step 2: Submit National ID, passport photo, and academic certificates
Step 3: Pay the application fee at the Finance Office
Step 4: Attend the admission interview (date communicated via email)
Step 5: Receive admission letter and report for orientation
📝 Registration Information
Registration Period: First 2 weeks of each semester
Required: Admission letter, fee receipt, and ID
Where: Front Office — collect registration form
Late Registration: Penalty fee applies after deadline
Contact: Front Office or Academic Registrar
📚 Exam Information
Exam Timetables: Posted on the noticeboard 2 weeks before exams
Requirements: Student ID card must be presented at exam hall
Special Needs: Notify Front Office at least 1 week before exams
Results: Published within 4 weeks of last exam
Appeals: Submit appeal form within 2 weeks of results
🎓 Orientation Guide
Orientation Date: First day of the academic year
Venue: Main Hall — check noticeboard for updates
What to Bring: Admission letter, ID, pen and notebook
Campus Tour: Guided tours available after orientation
Student ID: Collected at Front Office after registration
